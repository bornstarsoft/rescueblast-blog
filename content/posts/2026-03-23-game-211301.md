KONAMI is set to delight fans with its latest addition to the Picross series, titled **Picross S: Konami Antiques Collection**. Scheduled for release on April 30, this game will feature 585 puzzles inspired by over 80 classic Konami titles, including beloved franchises like *Castlevania*, *Metal Gear*, and *Contra*. Players will engage in a nostalgic journey through pixel art, solving puzzles that reveal iconic sprites from the rich history of Konami's gaming legacy.

This new installment promises a variety of puzzle types, including Mega Picross and Color Picross, catering to both casual gamers and seasoned puzzle enthusiasts. With the support of mouse controls and touchscreen capabilities on the Nintendo Switch, players can enjoy a seamless gaming experience. As the ninth entry in the Picross S series, it continues the trend of celebrating gaming history through engaging gameplay.

As we look forward to the release, one can't help but wonder: how will this new Picross game resonate with both longtime fans of Konami and newcomers alike? Will it spark a renewed interest in classic gaming through the lens of modern puzzle-solving?
