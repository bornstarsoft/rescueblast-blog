Get ready for a delightful adventure as ININ, in collaboration with Studio Siesta and Rocket Engine, announces the release of Trouble Witches FINAL! Episode 01 Daughters of Amalgam. Set to launch on December 15 for Nintendo Switch and PlayStation 4, this game promises to bring back the charm of bullet-hell shooters with a fresh twist. Fans can also grab the Physical Special Edition starting December 9, packed with exclusive goodies that make it a must-have for collectors.

Originally released in 2007, Trouble Witches has undergone significant evolution to become the ultimate Cute Em Up experience. With 12 unique witches to choose from, each boasting distinctive abilities and attack styles, players can dive into exhilarating shoot em up action. The game features a vibrant visual overhaul and a unique combat system that allows players to convert enemy bullets into gold coins, enhancing both strategy and gameplay.

As we look forward to the release, its exciting to see how Trouble Witches FINAL! will appeal to both casual gamers and hardcore bullet-hell enthusiasts alike. Will this new installment redefine the Cute 'Em Up genre? Only time will tell!
