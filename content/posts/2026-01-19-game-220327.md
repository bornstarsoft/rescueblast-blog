Sega is gearing up for an exciting celebration as Puyo Puyo approaches its 35th anniversary in 2026. The company has unveiled the Dairensa! project, named after a beloved song from the game series, promising a slew of commemorative activities that will delight fans around the world. With a special live stream scheduled for February 1, 2026, featuring voice actors and live demos, Sega is set to engage its community like never before.

In addition to the live stream, fans can look forward to a Puyo Puyo pop-up store in Japan, along with collaborative events that merge the worlds of Sonic and Puyo Puyo. From limited edition merchandise to exciting lottery prizes, Sega is ensuring that this anniversary is one for the books. As the gaming community anticipates these celebrations, it raises the question: what other surprises does Sega have in store for its loyal fans?
