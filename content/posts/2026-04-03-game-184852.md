Apple Arcade has just added two remarkable indie titles that are sure to captivate gamers: Dredge and Unpacking. Both games have been meticulously optimized for mobile devices, making them more accessible than ever. Dredge+ offers a unique blend of fishing and horror, immersing players in a chilling narrative where they must navigate the waters by day and face Lovecraftian horrors by night. Unpacking+, on the other hand, transforms the simple act of arranging items into a heartfelt experience, showcasing the emotional journey of moving into a new home.

Dredge+ stands out with its complete edition, featuring all downloadable content that enhances the gameplay experience. The fishing mechanics are engaging, and the psychological twists keep players on their toes. Unpacking+ has received accolades for its cultural impact, proving that even the simplest gameplay can resonate deeply with players. Both titles exemplify the creativity and innovation found in indie games, making them must-plays for Apple Arcade subscribers.
