The upcoming pixel art block-pushing puzzle game, Hazard Pay, is set to launch this June for PlayStation 5, Nintendo Switch, and PC via Steam. Developed by Smitner Studio and published by Numskull Games, this game promises to deliver "ingenious, compact, handcrafted" puzzle levels that will challenge players' problem-solving skills in a visually appealing environment.

Currently, a demo is available for PC users, allowing gamers to get a taste of the unique mechanics and charming aesthetics before the official release. With its engaging gameplay and retro-inspired graphics, Hazard Pay is poised to attract both puzzle enthusiasts and fans of pixel art alike. Will this game redefine the puzzle genre, or will it simply be another title in the vast sea of indie games? Only time will tell.
