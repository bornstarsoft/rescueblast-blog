Microsoft has just unveiled an exciting lineup of 13 new titles coming to Xbox Game Pass this May, including the much-anticipated Forza Horizon 6, set to release on May 19. This announcement marks a significant moment for Xbox, especially following their recent price drop for Game Pass Ultimate, making it more accessible for gamers worldwide.

Among the new additions, players can look forward to titles like Mixtape and Subnautica 2, which promise to deliver engaging gameplay experiences across Xbox, PC, and cloud streaming. With such a diverse selection, from racing to adventure, Xbox Game Pass continues to solidify its position as a leading gaming subscription service.
