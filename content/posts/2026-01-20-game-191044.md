Astro Loopers is an innovative 4D puzzle game that brilliantly merges classic Sokoban-style gameplay with the intriguing mechanics of time loops and unique character skills. Players are invited to harness the power of their past selves, repeating actions to navigate through a series of stages while collecting the elusive Fragments of Time scattered across the universe. This combination of strategy and creativity sets Astro Loopers apart in the crowded gaming landscape.

Each character in Astro Loopers comes equipped with distinct abilities that interact with blocks in various wayswhether breaking, pushing, or pulling them. This adds layers of complexity to the puzzles, requiring players to master each characters skill to overcome the diverse challenges presented in over 150 stages. From slippery ice floors to explosive blocks, the game offers a rich tapestry of mechanics that keep players engaged and thinking critically.

The demo version of Astro Loopers is available for free, allowing players to experience 24 stages of this captivating adventure. As you embark on this journey through time and space, you'll find yourself not just solving puzzles but also enjoying the freedom to stream and monetize your gameplay. Will you rise to the challenge and conquer the mind-bending puzzles that await?
