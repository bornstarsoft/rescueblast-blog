PC gamers can now dive into the colorful world of Nested, an enchanting puzzle game that recently released a free demo. This game, created by Nathan Sioui as part of the GMTK 2024 Game Jam, stands out with its whimsical aesthetic and clever mechanics that challenge players to stack nesting dolls in a variety of engaging ways.

Nested invites players to solve puzzles by matching the correct sizes and colors of nesting dolls, providing a cozy yet stimulating experience. As you progress through the game, you'll encounter unique mechanics like color-inverting buttons and swap nodes that keep gameplay fresh and exciting. The demo is available for download on Steam, allowing players to experience its charm firsthand.

With a full release expected in August 2026, fans of indie games and puzzle enthusiasts alike should keep an eye on Nested. Will this delightful game capture your heart and challenge your mind?
