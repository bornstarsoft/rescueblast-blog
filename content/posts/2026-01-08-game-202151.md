The launch of AiliA, a captivating mirror-based puzzle game developed by IceToad Studio LLC, marks an exciting addition to the gaming landscape. Now available on Steam for $11.99, with a 20% discount for early buyers, this game invites players to delve into a world where reality and reflection intertwine. The unique gameplay mechanics, centered around interactive mirrors, offer a fresh take on puzzle-solving that is sure to engage both casual and hardcore gamers alike.

In AiliA, players step into the shoes of Aria, navigating through beautifully crafted stages that blur the lines between the real world and a mirrored realm. The puzzles are not just visually stimulating; they challenge players to think critically about how they manipulate their environment, making each solution a rewarding experience. With over 100 puzzles spread across seven distinct themed areas, AiliA promises hours of immersive gameplay.

For those who enjoy a good deal, a special bundle featuring AiliA and the game Moncage is also available, providing an additional 10% discount. Plus, a free demo allows players to experience the enchanting world of AiliA before committing to a purchase. As we look ahead, it will be interesting to see how players respond to this innovative approach to puzzle gaming. Will AiliA redefine the genre?
