This December, PlayStation Plus subscribers have a lot to look forward to with the latest additions to the Game Catalog. Notably, the highly anticipated titles, Assassins Creed Mirage and Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty, are set to make their debut, promising thrilling gameplay experiences for both PS5 and PS4 users. Additionally, the inclusion of Skate Story, which is already available, adds to the excitement for gamers eager to explore new adventures.

In addition to these major titles, the Classic Games Catalog welcomes the beloved Soulcalibur III, a nostalgic fighter that will surely delight long-time fans. With a diverse lineup that also features family-friendly options like Paw Patrol games and Lego Horizon Adventures, this months offerings cater to a wide range of gaming preferences.
