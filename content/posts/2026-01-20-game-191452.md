Astro Loopers is revolutionizing the puzzle game genre by blending classic Sokoban-style gameplay with innovative time loops and unique character skills. This fresh 4D puzzle game invites players to harness the power of their past selves, allowing them to repeat actions and collaborate with their team to navigate through over 150 stages filled with challenging puzzles and mechanics. 

Each character in Astro Loopers possesses a distinctive ability that interacts with blocks in various wayswhether breaking, pushing, or pulling them. Mastering these skills is crucial for solving intricate puzzles that range from simple to mind-bending challenges. With diverse mechanics like slippery ice floors and explosive blocks, players must think creatively to conquer each stage. 

The demo for Astro Loopers is currently available for free, allowing players to experience 24 stages of this captivating adventure. As you embark on this journey through time and space, will you be able to collect all the Fragments of Time? The challenge awaits!
